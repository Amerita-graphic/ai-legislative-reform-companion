3. How the Document Workflow Works

When you submit a document above, it triggers a five-step AI pipeline in Relay.app that processes the legislation and delivers a complete analysis package by email.

  1. Webhook Trigger — Form submission sends document text to Relay.app via secure webhook
  2. Generate Legislative Brief — AI produces executive summary, key provisions, enforcement gaps, and recommended amendments
  3. Generate Cross‑Walk — AI maps evidence to clauses to proposed amendments in a structured table
  4. Extract Numeric Data — AI extracts all penalties, fees, thresholds, and timeframes into a dataset
  5. Generate Visual Explainer — AI produces Canva-ready infographic design instructions
  6. Email Delivery — All outputs delivered to inbox automatically

Sample Output — Legislative Brief

=== LEGISLATIVE BRIEF === This Act establishes basic tobacco controls including smoke-free public places, health warnings on packs, a ban on sales to children, inspection powers, and some advertising restrictions. However, the rules are limited and the penalties are low, which may reduce deterrence and make compliance uneven. ENFORCEMENT GAPS The packaging warning covers only 30% of the front panel with no graphic warnings or back-of-pack requirements. The advertising ban leaves loopholes for sponsorship, digital media, and point-of-sale promotion. Penalties are too low to deter large corporate operators. RECOMMENDED AMENDMENTS • Increase pack warnings to 75% front and back with graphic images • Replace partial advertising restrictions with a comprehensive ban • Raise penalties with escalating fines for repeat offences • Add enforcement powers: seizure, compliance notices, prosecution • Introduce retailer licensing with suspension for repeated violations
